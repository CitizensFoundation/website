The Serbian state in collaboration with the United Nations Development Program are crowdsourcing ideas from the citizens to reform Serbia’s business environment, using Your Priorities.

“We are interested in your opinion on how regulations affect digital economy and entrepreneurship. Contribute to a better environment for the business of the technology sector.”

[Visit site](https://adminhack.yrpri.org/community/557).

This project is in cooperation with our partners in the [Democratic Society](http://www.demsoc.org/).
