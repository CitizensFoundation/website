We, the Citizens Foundation, did two presentations at the Digital Cities conference in Athens, Greece during the last weekend. They were both well received and referred to in 5 other lectures and/or Q&As that came after our presentations. Both as interesting in connection to those lectures subjects as well as in terms of seeing interesting possibilities in interfaceing with those subjects.

We also had interesting discussions with various people from many countries regarding collaboration on projects. And of course continous brainstorming amongst our team, not the least on the Acropolis itself overlooking the Agora 🙂

All in all, a wonderful trip which we’ll surely be reaping benefits from in years to come!
