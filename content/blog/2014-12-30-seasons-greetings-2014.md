#### **A very merry seasons greetings to all**   

#### from us at the Citizens Foundation in Iceland!

#### Highlights from 2014

-   ****In 2014 we presented and participated with good people**** in many countries at conferences, meetings, workshops, crowd-sourcing budgets processes, collaborators, partners in democratic innovation and more. Thank you all!

-   We received the brilliant news that ****7 Estonian law proposals crowd-sourced through Your Priorities**** ****have become laws****, improving democracy and grassroots influence in Estonia – [http://www.citizens.is/portfolio/estonian-laws-changed/](http://www.citizens.is/portfolio/estonian-laws-changed/)

-   We released a new 3D add-on to Your Priorities that uses virtual worlds and irc chat to ****connect people better together online –**** [http://spjall.betrireykjavik.is/](http://spjall.betrireykjavik.is/)

-   We continued to make ****Your Priorities more user-friendly**** on any platform you want to use, this is our favorite never-ending process 🙂 – [https://yrpri.org](https://yrpri.org/). We launched a preview of the new version to be released in 2015 – [http://your-priorities-preview.org/](http://your-priorities-preview.org/)

-   We worked with the NHS of UK to ****improve their communication with their clients –**** [https://nhs-citizen.yrpri.org/](https://nhs-citizen.yrpri.org/)

-   We continued working on our annual ****participatory budgeting project –**** [https://www.betrireykjavik.is/pages/6-betri-hverfi-2015](https://www.betrireykjavik.is/pages/6-betri-hverfi-2015) ****–**** and also continued our work on improving Better Reykjavik, both in co-operation with the City of Reykjavik – [https://www.betrireykjavik.is/](https://www.betrireykjavik.is/)

-   We partnered with the Icelandic municipalities Hafnarfjörður and Hvalfjarðarsveit in using citizens participation to make the community better – [https://betrihafnarfjordur.is/](https://betrihafnarfjordur.is/)
