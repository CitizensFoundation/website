From 27th to 29th of September, 2013, Robert Bjarnason and Gunnar Grímsson, as representatives of the Citizens Foundation, participated in the 2nd South Eastern European Government Communication Conference (SEECOM) which took place in Budva, Montenegro.

The SEECOM is basically a collective effort of government communicators from nine countries of South Eastern Europe to promote greater government openness, more transparency and greater public participation in policy making.

The culmination of the conference was the signing of the Open Government Declaration. It was a great honor to be among the signers of a historic declaration of commitment to the principles of open government.
