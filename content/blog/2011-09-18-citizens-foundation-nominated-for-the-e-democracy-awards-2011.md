Citizens Foundation, which runs Better Reykjavík and Your Priorities, has been nominated for European eDemocracy Award 2011 which is a part of World eGov Forum 2011.

The nominees will present their projects at the Awards Ceremony in Issy, Paris in the beginning of October.
