Sorry about the meta title but it’s really what it is. An Estonian law about citizens petitions came into the [Rahvakogu](http://www.citizens.is/portfolio/estonian-laws-changed/) through [Your Priorities](https://yrpri.org/home/world) and was approved by the Estonian parliament last year. And now the Estonian parliament has approved a law that came into being through a citizens petition. That’s one real step further in citizens empowerment through Your Priorities.
