We had a great experience doing a presentation at The Icelandic Youth Councils conference. We presented our work on using e-democracy increase participation, especially with young people. The audience once again confirmed our belief that young people are not less but more capable than most of their elders, bombarding us with difficult questions which we did our best to answer. Thank you!
