Over 100.000 people have visited the True Stories website, approximately 10.000 people have either submitted a story, commented or voted and 767 stories were submitted by EvE Online.

CCP took the topmost stories and presented them to the writer of True Stories,Daniel Way. After the discussion the Band of Brothers story was chosen.

Now the first comic book is already released. It is a 64 – page comic book, split up into four 16- page chapters. Each chapter illustrated by a separate artist and cover artist. First one was published on 19 February 2014, with the next issues appearing every two weeks.
